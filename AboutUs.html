Our Mission

We love doing these podcasts. Our goal is to spread positivity throughout our community. Forever Positive™ was created based on the idea that everyone has a rough time throughout their lives. We needed someone to help us get through dark, and rough patches of our lives. We want to be those people. The people you can hit play and go throughout your day and hopefully be in a better mood, a happier you. This podcast is a judgement free zone! We support ALL communities and we do not judge. We all live on this earth, so why not help others out since we all are on the same boat, and the same goal, to live a good life. :)
