Frequently Asked Questions

What Is Premium and Premium Hub??

A: Premium is a NEW service created by Forever Positive. Premium members pay a small payment per month or yearly for the service. Premium offers many unique services! Such as Extra podcasts, videos, documentaries, Free books, and More! Premium Hub is a special page for Premium listeners only. It is a password protected page.

Why can't I acess the Premium Hub directly after purchase?

A: Once a customer purchases one of the two offered Premium plans, it can take up to 24 hours to be emailed with your account information. If it has exceeded 24 hours, please email us, and a human will send you your code.

My password no longer works?

A: There are two possible scnarios: 1. Your password may be incorrect. The sign-in is sensitive to capital and lowercase letters. You must enter your password exactly as your confirmation email says.
2. You may have not renewed your Premium membership. In this case, you will need to renew your membership, and your password will automatically re-activate after 24 hours.

Can I share my account?

A: NO! Sharing your Premium account with non-subscribed customers will result in an extermination of your account permanatly, and a refund will NOT be issued!.
